About Me

Currently, I am an Embedded System Engineer-Vehicle Software, at Yatri Motorcycles . I received my Bachelor's degree in Electronics andCommunication Engineering from the Institute of Engineering, Pulchowk Campus, Tribhuvan University. I am seeking a research assistant position to work on embedded and computer science.
During my college days, I was a member of Robotics Club inside the college. Joining this club helped me boost my confidence and accelerate my skills in multiple fields.
Besides, I am also a decent basketball player and have played in multiple tournaments during my college days. Additionally, I enjoy doing free writing, painting, and reading novels.

I joined Yatri Motorcycles, as an intern, during my last semester of undergrad. I was assigned an independent project named OTA (Over the Air Update), where the firmware in the bike had to be updated wirelessly. I created a simple prototype of this project during my intern period and have been working on it as a full-time embedded vehicle software engineer after my finals. So far, the complete system update firmware is completed and ready for deployment.
Besides my solo project, I am also working on multiple other, such as creating an internal server for charging stations, OBD (onboard diagnosis) firmware flash, and more.

I was involved in the Robotics Club, Pulchowk Campus, which participates in the annual ABU Robocon, an Asian-Oceanian college robot competition. Here, I learned PCB designing, printing, and fabricating the circuits, along with some basics of programming. This experience inspired me to do various other projects related to robotics.
Furthermore, joining this club was immensely challenging for me due to the abysmal number of girls, along with no affiliation for the stay and lavatory for girls.

While I was in my final years, I started learning Neural Networks. After taking a few courses and completing mini-projects, we did our Final Year Project using Deep Neural Networks. This started my interest in AI and machine learning as well.
In addition to that, I occasionally write blogs specifying the little snags during a project. Sometimes I do poetry too. I am also fascinated by the arts and literature. I enjoy outdoor activities like hiking, playing sports, sightseeing, and more. I am also an animal lover and a vegetarian.
